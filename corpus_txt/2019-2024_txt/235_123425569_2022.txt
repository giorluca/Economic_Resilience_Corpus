In other words, the ARDL approach can be applied regardless of whether the underlying regressors are integrated of order one [I(1)], order zero [I(0)] or fractionally integrated. Thirdly, the ARDL technique generally provides unbiased estimates of the long-run model and valid t-statistics even when some of the regressors are endogenous  (Odhiambo, 2009, p. 219).
