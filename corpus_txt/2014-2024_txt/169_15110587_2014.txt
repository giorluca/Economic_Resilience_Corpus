thicknessPresent Work: Design Optimisation Overall Conclusions• New Zealand milk powder plants can economically increase HR by ~20 %; exhaust heat recovery (HR) is the single largest opportunity• Exhaust HR can play an integral part in supplying heat to neighbouring plants via HRLs• For SMP, a final exhaust air temperature above 55 °C can minimise particulate fouling problems• Exhaust HR is
